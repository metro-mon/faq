Frequently Asked Questions

1. What version is the server on?

The server is on Forge 1.16.5, so please use the Forge modloader to join!

2. How do we setup our Minecraft Account to join the server?

How to setup:

- Go to this link and under the Download Recommended portion, click the big installer button.
- After that, you have to skip the ad in the installer page to download the installer jar. Once it is done downloading, click the jar and go to Install Client then press OK.
- Now, launch Minecraft under the new instance. Download the MetroMon Modpack and unzip it in the mods folder.
- Now, you can connect to the server under the server address(you can find it in our discord) and play with us! Enjoy!

3. How do we join the discord and what do we do in the discord?

The discord is where you'll be able to talk with other people joining the server, as well as on other things like the Pokemon games. There's also a currency system as well as bots that allow you to play a few games. Here is the link: https://discord.gg/fP7CxUAAn2
